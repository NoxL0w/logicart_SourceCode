Returns, Exchange, and Warranty Policy

To benefit from Return, Exchange, and Warranty services, the original invoice is required.

Eligible Items:

  • All items are subject to Return and Exchange except software, unless a manufacturing defect is found.
  • Products must be in their original packaging and in unused condition.

Return and Exchange Period:

  • Returns: within 3 days of purchase.
  • Exchanges: within 7 days of purchase.

Conditions:

  • Promotional/free items must also be returned in original condition or their value will be deducted.
  • Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used at checkout.
  • Products purchased via partner vouchers or installment are refundable as store credit only.
  • Return and Exchange are valid only within the country of purchase.
  • Service and shipping charges are non-refundable.

Online Purchases:

  • Accepting the delivery confirms that the product box is sealed and in good condition.
  • Return/exchange window starts from the delivery date.
  • If no Logicart showroom is nearby, please contact us through our support line and a courier will be arranged for pickup.
  • International shipping fees, taxes, and duties are non-refundable unless the product has a manufacturing defect.
  • International orders are not eligible for return/exchange unless defective. Contact us at +639XXXXXXXX for assistance.

Immediate Replacement for Defective Items:

  • Logicart will replace any monitor, CPU, or peripheral with a manufacturing (non-software) defect within 3 days of purchase, provided the following:
    • The device has no scratches or signs of misuse.
    • Original box, manuals, and accessories are returned in full.
    • A technical inspection may be required before replacement.
